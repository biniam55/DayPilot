# DayPilot Feature Guide

## 🎯 New Features Overview

### 1. Subtasks & Progress Tracking

**What you'll see:**
- Tasks with subtasks show a **progress bar** below the task name
- Example: "Project presentation prep" shows "1/3 subtasks (33%)"
- Click the **chevron icon (▶)** next to the task name to expand and see all subtasks
- Each subtask has a checkbox showing completion status

**How to identify:**
- Look for the 📋 icon and "X/Y subtasks" text
- Progress bar shows visual completion percentage
- Expandable arrow appears on tasks with subtasks

**Example in sample data:**
- "Project presentation prep" has 3 subtasks
- "Gym session" has 4 subtasks for workout structure

---

### 2. Energy Level Indicators

**What is Energy Level?**
Energy level helps you match tasks to your mental/physical state:

#### 🔥 High Energy (Red/Orange)
- **When:** Morning (9am-12pm) - You're fresh and alert
- **Tasks:** Complex problem-solving, creative work, strategic planning
- **Examples:** 
  - Writing important code
  - Designing presentations
  - Making critical decisions
  - Learning new skills

#### ⚡ Medium Energy (Yellow)
- **When:** Midday (12pm-3pm) - Steady energy
- **Tasks:** Regular work, meetings, collaboration
- **Examples:**
  - Team meetings
  - Planning sessions
  - Routine development work
  - Organizing projects

#### 🌙 Low Energy (Blue/Purple)
- **When:** Afternoon (3pm-6pm) - Energy dips
- **Tasks:** Simple, repetitive work
- **Examples:**
  - Checking email
  - Admin tasks
  - Filing documents
  - Simple reviews

**Why it matters:**
- Doing hard work when tired = poor results
- Doing easy work when energized = wasted potential
- Match task to energy = maximum productivity

**Where to see it:**
- Small emoji next to the priority badge on each task
- "Project presentation prep" shows 🔥 (needs high energy)
- "Email review" shows 🌙 (can do when tired)

---

### 3. Recurring Tasks

**What you'll see:**
- Tasks that repeat show a **🔄 badge** with the frequency
- Badge text: "🔄 daily", "🔄 weekly", or "🔄 monthly"

**Examples in sample data:**
- "Email review" - 🔄 daily (do every day)
- "Weekly Sync" - 🔄 weekly (every week)
- "Gym session" - 🔄 daily (daily routine)

**Use cases:**
- Daily: Morning routine, email check, exercise
- Weekly: Team meetings, weekly review, reports
- Monthly: Monthly reports, reviews, planning

---

### 4. Time Tracking

**What you'll see:**
- Every task shows estimated time: "60m"
- Completed tasks show actual time: "30m (actual: 25m)"
- This helps you learn how long tasks really take

**Example:**
- "Email review" shows: "30m (actual: 25m)"
- This means: Estimated 30 minutes, actually took 25 minutes
- Over time, you'll get better at estimating

**Benefits:**
- Learn your actual speed
- Improve future estimates
- Avoid overcommitting
- Better schedule planning

---

## 🔍 How to See All Features

### Option 1: Click "Reset to Sample Data" Button
1. Look for the button in the **bottom-right corner**
2. Click "Reset to Sample Data"
3. Page will refresh with new sample tasks showing all features

### Option 2: Create a New Task with All Features
1. Go to "My Planner" view
2. In the task input:
   - Type task name
   - Select Priority: 🔴 High, 🟡 Medium, or 🟢 Low
   - Choose Duration: 15 min to 3 hours
   - Pick Energy: 🔥 High, ⚡ Medium, or 🌙 Low
   - Set Repeat: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or No repeat
3. Press Enter or click "Add"

## 🎓 Best Practices

### Morning Routine (High Energy 🔥)
1. Review high-priority tasks
2. Tackle most complex work first
3. Schedule 2-3 hour blocks for deep work
4. Example: "Write proposal", "Design architecture"

### Midday (Medium Energy ⚡)
1. Meetings and collaboration
2. Regular development work
3. Planning and organizing
4. Example: "Team sync", "Code review"

### Afternoon (Low Energy 🌙)
1. Email and communication
2. Admin tasks
3. Simple reviews
4. Example: "Email review", "Update documentation"

### Using Subtasks
1. Break large tasks into 15-30 min chunks
2. Make each subtask actionable
3. Check off as you complete
4. Track progress visually

### Setting Recurrence
- **Daily:** Habits you do every day
- **Weekly:** Regular meetings, reviews
- **Monthly:** Reports, planning sessions

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the new features"
**Solution:** Click "Reset to Sample Data" button (bottom-right)

### "My old tasks don't have energy levels"
**Solution:** Edit existing tasks to add energy levels and other features

### "Subtasks aren't showing"
**Solution:** 
1. Make sure task has subtasks (check sample data)
2. Click the ▶ arrow to expand
3. Look for "Project presentation prep" or "Gym session"

### "Progress bar is missing"
**Solution:** Only tasks with subtasks show progress bars

4. **Track time:** Learn your actual speed
5. **Set recurring:** Automate routine tasks
